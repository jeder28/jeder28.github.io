About

I’ve always been an artsy person with a passion for technology. So as a web develper, good design is very important to me. I love making web applications with the user always in mind. For me, UX is like storytelling, beginning with easy to use functionality, enhancing that experience through its visual design, then growing into an app with use and purpose. Other facts about me include my love of cooking and baking, especially chicken pot pie. I also love DIY projects, such as laying new tile in my kitchen, and more crafty projects, like creating the reception decorations for my wedding this past June or crocheting scarves and blankets.

There's A Board

Date: Oct 2015 Roles: Front-End, Back-End

There's a board is an internal app designed to encourage and aid Dev Bootcamp students to tutor by scheduling their time, providing goals to tutor a number of times and promote friendly competition between students on the number of times they tutored.


  • Front-End: Custom Javascript
  • Back-end: Rails, Rails Api
  • Plugins: jQuery fullCalendar
  • Templates: Bootstrap
  • Deployed: Heroku

Stack Overphil

Date: Oct 2015 Roles: Built Custom CSS for Front End, Back-End

A mini-version of Stack Overflow that allows our users to ask and answer questions, decide which answer to their questions are best and vote on any questions, answers or comments. This data is sortable by popularity, recently posted or tags.


  • Front-End: All custom CSS
  • Back-end: Rails
  • Technique: Fast sorting with AJAX
